For over fifty years, Precept has been training leaders, creating resources, and developing a Bible study method to help people grow in their relationship with God—including you!

Our goal is to help you—and millions like you—engage in relationship with God through knowing His Word. So how does it work?

Precept Bible studies are based on three Essentials. These are foundational to everything we do and make up the “secret sauce” that sets us apart from other Bible studies.

  1. Method and Materials

    2. With a unique and time-tested method of study—and more than 250 unique titles covering every book of the Bible—Precept has a Bible study to meet you where you are.

  2. Individual Study

    3. Take the time to pray, reflect, and reason through Scripture on your own (before meeting with a group) and let the Holy Spirit teach and transform you. We want you to know God personally!

  3. Group discussion

    4. Meeting with a group provides the opportunity to be affirmed or even challenged as others share different perspectives and respond to guiding questions from your Precept Leader.
