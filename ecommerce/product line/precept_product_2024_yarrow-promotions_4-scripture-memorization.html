“For the word of God is living and active, sharper than any two-edged sword, piercing to the division of soul and of spirit, of joints and of marrow, and discerning the thoughts and intentions of the heart.”

Hebrews 4:12

There’s nothing like memorizing scripture to ground you in God’s Word!

Here are 3 tips to help you this summer:

  1. Match your learning style: Are you a visual learner? Auditory? Think about what you do to remember phone numbers or details and apply the same approach to memorizing Scripture.
  2. Be accountable: ask a friend or spouse to memorize with you.
  3. Get creative: Keep Scripture in front of you with our Scripture memory cards and bracelets from Yarrow.
