What a weekend, %%FirstName%%!

We hope you had a wonderful time at the Gospel Coalition Women’s conference! We loved meeting you and worshiping with you.

If you haven’t already, download your free Bible study here.

Yarrow Bible studies are designed to help you discover truth in Scripture on your own, so you can make connections and welcome change, from the inside out.

As the old saying goes, “If you give a man a fish, you feed him for a day. If you teach a man to fish, you feed him for a lifetime.”

In other words, at Yarrow, we don’t want to just tell you what the Bible says. We want to guide you as you learn how to study it yourself!

As you get to know God’s Word, you’ll get to know Him.

Plus, you’ll grow in your ability to connect the Bible to your actual life.

The truth is, we all have questions about real-life stuff, and it’s so important to know how to find answers in the Bible for ourselves.

That way, we’ll not only grow in our relationship with God, but we’ll also become more like Him.

We’re so glad you’re joining us on this journey! Let’s dig deeper together.

Rooted in Him,
